A Centralia Original

Family-owned. Community loved. Over half a century in the making.

Bill & Bea's Drive-In has been a fixture of Centralia, Washington since the early 1970s — a place where the burgers are made fresh, the milkshakes come in a dozen flavors, and the staff knows your name.

Whether you're swinging through for a quick espresso in the morning or settling in for a full burger basket with the family, you'll find the same honest, no-fuss food that's kept locals coming back for generations.

We're not a chain. We're your neighbor.
