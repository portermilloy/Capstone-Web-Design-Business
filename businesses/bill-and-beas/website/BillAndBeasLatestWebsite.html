Our Story

A Centralia Original

Family-owned since the 1970s. Loved by generations of locals.

Bill & Bea's Drive-In has been part of Centralia's fabric for more than half a century. It's the kind of place where the burgers are made fresh, the milkshakes come in fifteen flavors, and the person at the window probably knows your order already.

Whether you swing through for a morning espresso on the way to work, or settle in for a full burger basket with the family on a Saturday, you'll find the same no-fuss food that's kept people coming back across three generations.

We're not a chain. We're your neighbor.
