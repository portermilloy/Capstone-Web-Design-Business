Process

How it works

Four steps from first conversation to finished site.

1
We talk

I want to know about your business, your customers, and what you actually need the site to do. This part usually takes about 30 minutes.

2
I design

I put together a design built around your business. Colors, fonts, and structure that fit what you do, not a generic layout I pulled off the shelf.

3
I build it

Hand-coded HTML and CSS. It loads fast, looks right on every screen, and you actually own the files when we're done.

4
You get it

I walk you through the finished site, answer your questions, and help you get it online. No mystery, no ongoing dependency on me.

About

Who I am

I'm Porter, a web developer from Centralia. I started building websites for local businesses after noticing that a lot of good businesses in this area either had no website or one that didn't do them justice.

I write every site by hand. No drag-and-drop builders, no templates, no monthly platform fees. You get a custom site and the actual files to go with it.

I work with businesses in Lewis County because that's home. I want to see the places here do well.
